FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. How long does the process take?

On average the process takes 30 days for your account to be ready, once the account is ready we start the application process. Sometimes we are able to get you in sooner, however, please makes sure this time frame is suitable for you.

2. How do I qualify for service?

You must have a source of income.

3. What are acceptable proof of income to get approved for a apartment/house?

Pay stubs, bank statements, letter of employment are all acceptable forms to verify your income. If you have to prove your income another way, you will need to contact the leasing office of the property you’re interested in renting to ask if your form of income verification is acceptable.

4. Will you find a House/Apartment for me?

We don’t offer property locating service, but there are plenty of companies that offer that service.

5. Are there only certain areas and apartments I can choose from?

No. You are able to select any apartment you want! You are not limited by area or to low-end second chance apartments.

6. Do you only work with Renters in Georgia?

No. We work with renters throughout the U.S. Only state we do not offer service in is New York.

7. What if I moving from one state to another state?

You must have a source of income in order for us to assist with out of state moves.

8. What if I want a House?

We apply to houses as well, however, the fee is different. It’s $1190 if you want a house. We do not work with private owners or real estate agents working for private owners. Property management companies only.

9. What if the house/apartment I wanted is no longer available when my account is ready?

You can change or add new property selections at any time during the process.

10. Will the apartment be in my name?

Yes it will.

11. What if my spouse and I both have an eviction?

YIf you and your spouse have an eviction, only one person can apply or both will be required to pay service fees. However, second applicant will receive a $250 discount.

12. Will I have to pay application fees, administration fees, deposit, etc…?

Yes. You are responsible for all fees required for approval.

13. What if I don’t meet the income requirements?

If you don’t meet the income requirements required by your apartment, we will not be able to get you approved.

14. What if I can’t provide pay stubs?

You will have to utilize our No Doc Approval. Click on Purchase for more information.

15. What are your fees?

$950 for an Apartment and $1190 for a House. Please click on purchase for more fee information.

16. How and when do I pay?

Once you have qualified for our service you can click Purchase on our website to pay for your service. You must pre-pay for you service. No service will be completed unless full payment is received.

17. What’s the next step after I pay?

Once your payment is completed, you will receive a form to fill-out with your information, and upload your supporting documents. You will also receive a contract to sign via Docusign that explains our terms of service and our Money Back Guarantee.

18. Is this service Guaranteed?

Yes! We Guarantee to get you approved for 1 of your 3 apartment selections or your Money Back.

19. Do you work with Section 8 or Income based housing?

Unfortunately we do not.

20.Do I have to have good credit?

No.

21. Will you check my credit?

No we will never check your credit.

22. Can I sill qualify if I had a recent eviction?

Yes! It does not matter how new or old your eviction, broken lease, foreclosure or bankruptcy is, we will get you approved.

23. Would a recent bankruptcy disqualify me?p>

No it will not.
